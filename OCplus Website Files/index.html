Take Obby Creator to the next level

OC+ is an enhancement exploit for Obby Creator that provides new features and addons for the game.

Why OC+?

Simple

OC+ blends into Obby Creator for a smooth experience. Everything can be customized right at the Settings panel.

Feature-rich

OC+ houses a variety of features to be used with Obby Creator such as undo and redo with more to come.

Reliable

While compatibility isn't great, OC+ just works with any supported executors, with further support to come.
